Winmark Corporation | WINA

Consumer Cyclical

Specialty Retail

Winmark Corporation (NASDAQ:WINA) is a franchisor of five very different retail businesses. The company makes approximately half of its revenue from the ‘Plato’s Closet’ and ‘Once Upon A Child’ brands, which both operate in the business of buying and selling lightly used clothing and related accessories. Plato’s Closet caters more towards fashion trends for teenagers and young women, and competitors include American Eagle and Hollister. Once Upon A Child focuses on baby clothing and toys. The company is also the franchisor of ‘Play It Again Sports’, which involves the buying and selling of sports equipment.
